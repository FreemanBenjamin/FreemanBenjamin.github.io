3D Printer Specs

My current 3D printer is the CR-10s by Creality (pictured above) which I purchased in November 2017. It has a 300 x 300 x 400mm (12 x 12 x 14in) build platform with a heated glass bed. It prints via Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and has the capablility to print in layer heights of roughtly 0.04mm to 0.4mm. I am currently printing using Hatchbox 1.75mm diameter filament and preparing files using Slic3r. Future modifications will likely include stepper motor dampers to reduce noise and a new spool holder to replace the stock piece.
